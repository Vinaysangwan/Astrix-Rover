About Us

We are a passionate team of 20+ students from Amity University, united by our love for innovation and exploration! Hailing from diverse academic backgrounds, we have come together with a shared dream: to compete in prestigious Rover challenges around the globe. Our sights are set on the 2025-26 International Rover Challenge (IRC) and the University Rover Challenge (URC), where we aim to showcase our skills, creativity, and teamwork on the world stage.

The Rover

Our rover is engineered with advanced sensors, robust mobility systems, and artificial intelligence designed to navigate the challenging terrain of Mars. From high-resolution imaging to sample collection, every component is built for exploration.
