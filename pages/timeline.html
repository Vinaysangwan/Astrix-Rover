🚀 Project Timeline

Here's how the Astrix Rover project progresses month by month across planning, prototyping, and testing phases.

📌 Planning & Research

March – April 2024

Project planning, initial design ideas, and simulation testing were performed before physical prototyping.

🔧 Prototype & Testing

May – July 2024

Rapid prototyping and simulation, followed by functional testing of digital models and basic rover modules.

🧪 Integration & Development

August – October 2024

Subsystems were integrated. Advanced electronics, AI, and mechanical design were tested for real-world terrain and conditions.

🛠️ Refinement & Finalization

November – December 2024

System optimization, documentation, and final reviews take place before international submission and testing.
