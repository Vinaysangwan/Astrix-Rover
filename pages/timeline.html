🚀 Project Timeline

Here's how the Astrix Rover project progresses month by month across planning, prototyping, and testing phases.

📌 Planning

March 10 – March 23, 2025

Defined objectives, set milestones, and created the initial design concept for the rover project.

🔧 Prototype Development & Autonomous System Initiation

March 24 – April 13, 2025

Built a basic prototype and began work on the rover’s autonomous capabilities in parallel.

📝 First Draft – Detailed Plan

April 14 – April 20, 2025

Developed a refined plan including equipment lists, updated system architecture, and key component specs.

💰 Market Research & Cost Analysis

April 21 – April 27, 2025

Researched available components and finalized a comprehensive cost analysis to inform purchasing decisions.

🧪 Simulation & Testing

April 28 – May 4, 2025

Performed system simulations and tested early builds to evaluate performance and identify improvement areas.

📚 Exam Period

May 5 – May 31, 2025

Project activities were paused during this time for academic exams.

💡 Remote Work & Research

June – July 2025

Continued with remote learning, acquired new technical skills, and refined the rover's design and planning.

🛒 Equipment Purchase & Project Kick-Off

August 1 – August 15, 2025

Procured hardware and finalized system design based on prior testing and research insights.

🚀 Full-Scale Development Begins

August 16 – August 31, 2025

Started building the final rover model. Planning sessions were conducted for hardware assembly and software development.

⚙️ Subsystem Assembly

September 1 – September 15, 2025

Assembled mechanical subsystems, integrated components, and began building the rover chassis.

🔌 Electronics Integration

September 16 – September 30, 2025

Integrated electronics and autonomous modules, and began subsystem coordination testing.

🔗 System Integration

October 1 – October 15, 2025

Combined all major systems into a unified model and initiated full integration testing.

🧪 Final Testing

October 16 – October 31, 2025

Conducted rigorous testing and debugging to ensure functionality and reliability.

🔧 Refinement & Optimization

November 1 – November 15, 2025

Focused on design refinements, resolving system issues, and optimizing overall performance.

🔄 Iterative Testing

November 16 – November 30, 2025

Continued performance tuning with repeated hardware and software testing cycles.

✅ Final Review

December 1 – December 15, 2025

Completed all adjustments, performed final testing, and ensured system readiness for delivery.

🎉 Project Completion

December 16 – December 25, 2025

Finalized documentation, prepared project deliverables, and successfully wrapped up the Astrix Rover build.
