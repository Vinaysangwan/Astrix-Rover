About Us

We are a passionate team of 20+ students from Amity University, united by our love for innovation and exploration! Hailing from diverse academic backgrounds, we have come together with a shared dream: to compete in prestigious Rover challenges around the globe.

Our Mission

To design, build, and test a Mars Rover that reflects the spirit of student-led innovation. Our mission is to excel in real-world engineering and problem-solving while preparing for competitions like the IRC and URC.

What Drives Us

Curiosity. Grit. Imagination. These values push us to learn fast, build smart, and work together as one. Every sensor, wheel, and line of code tells our story of resilience and vision.
